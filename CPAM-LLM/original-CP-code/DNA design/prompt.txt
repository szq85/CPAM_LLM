Problem Description:

Constraint 1: Word Selection - All designed DNA sequences must be selected from a predefined word list;

Constraint 2: GC Content - The GC content of each DNA sequence must be exactly 50%;

Constraint 3: Inter-strand Orthogonality - The Hamming distance between any two distinct DNA sequences must be at least 4;

Constraint 4: Self-Orthogonality - The Hamming distance between any DNA sequence and its corresponding inverse complementary sequence must be at least 4;

Constraint 5: Optimization Objective - Design a set of 6 DNA sequences, each 8 bases long, that satisfy all the above constraints.
